Who We Are

The Meyer Companies is a family of companies that provide value-driven:

  1. Construction Services
    • Meyer Brothers Building Company
  2. Real Estate Development/Brokerage
    • Heise-Meyer, LLC

Our Mission

The Meyer Companies are dedicated to exceeding our customer’s expectations, through highly motivated, professional employees, concentrated on providing exceptional construction, brokerage, development, and property management services. This objective is achieved by focusing on value oriented, relationship based clientele.

Our Vision

The Meyer Companies work with a unified focus to achieve our goal of providing the best in all facets of real estate, construction and property maintenance services.  Our multiple-disciplined organization works to minimize cyclical trends and create crossover synergies to reduce market, geographical, and segment barriers.
