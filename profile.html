Founded in 1977

In 1977 Dave and Roger Meyer co-founded Meyer Brothers Building Company . Originally located in Blue Springs, Missouri, the Meyer brothers initially specialized in sales and the erection of metal buildings.

Today the Meyer Companies are a professionally operated firm, offering expertise in full-line construction and real estate management services to commercial, industrial, and institutional users.

Today a professionally operated firm

The Company now offices at the Business Exchange, a new 50,000 sq. ft. tilt-up office building constructed by the firm.

Previous projects include banks, schools, offices, manufacturing buildings, warehouse and distribution centers, automobile dealerships, shopping centers, health care facilities, churches, family life centers, professional training facilities, and many more.

The Meyer Companies work with its clients through the entire planning and construction process to occupancy.
